Most Needed

Items We're Always Looking For

These are the items our kids and families need most. All donations should be new or in excellent condition.

School Supplies

Backpacks, notebooks, pencils, crayons, folders, glue sticks, scissors, and rulers.

Clothing & Coats

Winter coats, gloves, hats, socks, underwear, shoes, and everyday clothing (all sizes).

Adaptive Equipment

Sensory items, adaptive seating, communication devices, and mobility equipment.

Food & Snacks

Individually packaged snacks, granola bars, juice boxes, and non-perishable food items.
