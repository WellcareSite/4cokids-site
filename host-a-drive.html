Host a Donation Drive

The kids and families we serve consistently need clothing, school supplies, jackets and coats, and adaptive equipment. Organizing a drive at your workplace, school, or community group is one of the most impactful ways to help.

How it works:

  • Sign up — Fill out the form below and we'll provide all the materials you need
  • Spread the word — Use our flyers and talking points to promote your drive
  • Collect donations — Set up bins and gather supplies from your community
  • Deliver — Bring donations to us or we can arrange a pickup
