Our Story

Founded by People Who Saw the Need

“When teachers started buying school supplies and winter coats with their own money, we knew something had to change. Over 10% of Colorado kids live in poverty — but together, we’re closing that gap.”

4 Colorado Kids

Founded in 2017 by local business owners, teachers, therapists, and advocates

What We Do

Four Ways We Help Kids Thrive

Every program is designed to remove barriers so children can focus on being kids — learning, growing, and dreaming.

Erase the Need

Brand-new backpacks filled with school supplies, clothing, and healthy snacks for kids who go without.

Sponsor a Smile

Remove financial barriers so kids can access field trips, summer camps, and after-school activities.

Adaptive Equipment

Specialized equipment for children with physical disabilities — giving them freedom to play, learn, and grow.

Community Support

Connecting families with resources, support services, and programs that strengthen their foundation.

Real Impact

Teachers Shouldn't Have to Choose

"95% of teachers spend their own money on classroom supplies — an average of $895 per year. With 1 in 7 Colorado kids facing hunger, many educators are the last safety net. We're here to change that."
