Teachers

Supporting Teachers while they Support Students

Erase the Need

With nearly one in seven Colorado kids living in poverty, many teachers and principals throughout the state are filling the gap out of their own pockets. According to a survey, teachers spend an average of $479 of their own money each year on classroom supplies.

4 Colorado Kids partners with local schools to provide essential supplies, clothing, and resources so that teachers can focus on what they do best — teaching.

Request Help

Are you a teacher or principal who needs support? Submit a request and we'll work to connect you with the resources you need.
