Volunteer with 4 Colorado Kids

Volunteer Opportunities

4 Colorado Kids has many amazing volunteer opportunities to help local kids and families. Check out our current openings below:

Social Media Volunteer

Help us spread the word about our mission and connect with supporters online.

  • Keeping the charity's social media channels updated with interesting stories — currently Facebook, YouTube and Instagram
  • Planning and scheduling social media content and posts
  • Researching, creating and uploading relevant content e.g. graphics, videos, photos, audio, albums
  • Identifying key influencers and organizations to interact with on social media
  • Encouraging others to comment on posts and share them with others
  • Attending some of the charity's events with a view to reporting on them
  • Liaising with staff and other volunteers to ensure consistency in messaging
  • Use social media to reach new audiences and engage potential supporters

Event Support Volunteer

Help organize and run donation drives, community events, and fundraisers throughout the year.

Supply Sorting & Distribution

Help sort donated items and prepare them for distribution to kids and families in need.
