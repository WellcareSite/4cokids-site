Why Volunteer With Us?

Every pair of hands makes a difference. Our volunteers are the backbone of 4 Colorado Kids — without them, we couldn't serve the thousands of children and families who count on us.

Ways you can help:

  • Sort & pack — Organize donated supplies into backpacks and care packages
  • Events — Help run donation drives, community events, and fundraisers
  • Delivery — Transport supplies to schools and families in need
  • Social media — Help spread the word and share our mission online
  • Administrative — Data entry, phone calls, and office support
