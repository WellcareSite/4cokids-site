Our Impact

Making a difference in our community

Who We Are

4 Colorado Kids was founded in June 2017 by a group of local business owners, teachers, pediatric therapists, parents and advocates, to help close the gap for local families and children living in poverty.

How We Help

Erase the Need

Provide clothing, basic necessities, healthy snacks, and brand-new backpacks filled with school supplies to kids in need.

Sponsor a Smile

Remove financial barriers and provide access to experiences and opportunities through our sponsorship programs.

Adaptive Equipment

Provide adaptive equipment to children with physical disabilities, giving them the freedom to play, learn and grow.

Community Support

Connect families with community resources, support services, and programs that help strengthen their foundation.

What We Achieve

Through the generosity of our donors and volunteers, we've been able to make a real impact in our community.
