For Teachers

Support for Your Classroom

With over 10% of Colorado kids living in poverty, 95% of teachers and principals are digging into their own pockets to fill the gap — spending an average of $895 a year on classroom supplies.

4 Colorado Kids partners with local schools to provide essential supplies, clothing, and resources so teachers can focus on teaching.

What we provide:

  • Brand-new backpacks filled with school supplies
  • Clothing and winter gear for students
  • Healthy snacks and basic necessities
  • Adaptive equipment for students with disabilities

For Families

Support for Your Family

According to the 2025 ALICE Report, 37% of Colorado households are struggling to cover basic needs. With 1 in 7 kids facing food insecurity, many families must often choose between paying bills and buying necessities for their children.

If you know a family who could use our help, or if you are a family in need, please fill out the request form below.
